## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: What is Cafe Pomo?**
A: Cafe Pomo is a focus timer app designed to help you stay productive using the Pomodoro Technique. Customize your study sessions with relaxing cafe backgrounds, track your progress, and earn rewards as you build your focus habits.

**Q: Is Cafe Pomo free to use?**
A: Yes! Cafe Pomo is free to download and use. Some premium features and backgrounds are available through in-app purchases or by watching ads.

### Timer & Sessions

**Q: How do I customize my timer duration?**
A: Tap the settings icon on the main screen to adjust your focus and break durations to match your preferred workflow.

**Q: What happens if I close the app during a session?**
A: Your session progress is saved locally. When you return, you can continue where you left off.

**Q: How do streaks work?**
A: Complete at least one focus session per day to maintain your streak. Your streak resets if you miss a day.

### Purchases & Premium

**Q: How do I restore my purchases?**
A: Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

**Q: I made a purchase but didn't receive it. What do I do?**
A: First, try restoring purchases. If the issue persists, contact us at humexerience.design@gmail.com with your purchase receipt.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app is crashing or not working properly. What should I do?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure you have the latest version installed
4. If the issue continues, contact us with details about the problem

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: All your data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will remove all your data. There is no cloud account to delete.
